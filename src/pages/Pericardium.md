## Overview

The **Pericardium** — literally "Heart Wrapper" — is the Heart's protector. In the five-phase framework ([WuXing.md](WuXing.md)) the [Heart](Heart.md) stands alone in Fire and the Pericardium is barely mentioned; in the six-organ meridian framework ([Jingmai.md](Jingmai.md)) the Pericardium is promoted to a full sixth Zang organ paired with the [San Jiao](SanJiao.md), and the resulting 12 channels become the symmetrical backbone of the acupuncture system. See the [5-vs-6 Zang reconciliation](index.md#the-5-vs-6-zang-reconciliation).

## Primary function

The Pericardium **shields the Heart** from physical and emotional pathogens. External Heat and emotional shocks that would otherwise strike the Heart (the Emperor, who must not be disturbed) are absorbed by the Pericardium first. In practice this means the Pericardium often gets treated where Western intuition would treat the heart — anxiety, palpitations from acute stress, emotional defensiveness.
